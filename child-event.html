Junior and Senior Aquarist


Now your children can get a fascinating glimpse behind-the-scenes at Aqua World an Underwater Zoo.

Choose from two fun-packed experiences and discover if your child is a Marine Biologist in the making:


Junior Aquarist - Rs. 150 per child



>For children 5 - 7 years old.
>Behind-the-scenes tour, meet an Aquarist, watch a shark feeding, assist in feeding the animals, ride on a Glass-Bottom Boat and much more.
>Advanced Bookings Required.




Senior Aquarist - Rs. 250 per child



>For children 8 years and above
>Cage Snorkeling experience: close encounters with thousands of aquatic animals.
>Behind-the-scenes tour, meet an Aquarist, watch a shark feeding, assist in feeding the animals, ride on a Glass-Bottom Boat and much more.
>Advanced Bookings Required.
